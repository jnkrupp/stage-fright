Mission



stage_fright is an app aiming to help people overcome stage panic and public speaking troubles through VR and voice practice. Our VR stage sets the user in a dark room with 1 mic and many terrifying creatures concentrating their gazes on the user. Behind the user is a timer and the user will practice their speech into the microphone within the ticking time frame. Our app also provides a voice analyzing system that gives helpful feedback to the user on their recorded speaking session.
